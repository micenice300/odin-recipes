pizza

is a traditional Italian dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients, baked at a high temperature

ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour, or as needed
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup very warm water (120 degrees F to 130 degrees F)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Combine 2 cups flour, salt, and yeast in a large bowl
  2. Stir in very warm water and oil until combined, then
  3. stir in just enough of remaining 1/2 cup flour to
  4. make a soft dough. Knead on a lightly floured surface until
  5. smooth and elastic, 4 to 6 minutes. Cover and let rest for 10 minutes.
